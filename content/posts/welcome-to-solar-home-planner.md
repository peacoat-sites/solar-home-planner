Planning to go solar is one of the best decisions a homeowner can make. Whether you're looking to cut electricity bills, add backup power during outages, charge your EV for free, or take your home completely off-grid ,  this site is your comprehensive planning resource.

## What You'll Find Here

**Solar Contractor Selection** ,  How to choose a trustworthy solar installer, what certifications to look for (NABCEP), questions to ask before signing anything, and red flags to avoid.

**Solar for Your Home Type** ,  Whether you have a flat roof, metal roof, tile roof, mobile home, condo, or a house with significant tree shading ,  we cover the specific considerations for every home type.

**Permits & HOA Rules** ,  Navigate the permit process, understand your rights if an HOA tries to block your installation, and learn about solar easement laws in your state.

**DIY Solar** ,  For the hands-on homeowner: how to safely size and install your own system, the best DIY kits, ground mount options, and what you legally need a licensed electrician for.

**Solar + EV Charging** ,  How many panels to charge your electric vehicle, bidirectional charging explained, and how to optimize your combined solar + EV setup.

**Off-Grid & Backup Power** ,  Everything from Powerwall sizing to full off-grid system design, generator alternatives, and preparing for extended outages.

**Monitoring & Maintenance** ,  Track your system's performance, troubleshoot issues, and know when (and how) to clean your panels.
