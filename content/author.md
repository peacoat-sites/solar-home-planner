**Liz & Tom Greer** are the editors of Solar Home Planner. They review and curate all content published on this site, drawing on their firsthand experience designing, permitting, and installing a 9.2kW rooftop solar system on their own home in 2021.

Their focus is on making residential solar installation information accurate, practical, and easy to understand for homeowners who are navigating the process for the first time.

All articles are reviewed for accuracy before publication.
