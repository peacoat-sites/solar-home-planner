## Our Editorial Standards

Solar Home Planner exists to give readers accurate, practical, and genuinely useful information. This page explains how our content is made — and how we hold ourselves accountable for it.

## How we research and write

Every article begins with research into reputable, authoritative sources on the topic at hand. To cover subjects thoroughly and keep our library current, our team uses modern writing tools — including AI-assisted drafting — as part of the research and drafting process.

We're transparent about that because we believe it matters: **no article is published as raw, unedited machine output.** Every piece is structured, edited, and checked by a human editor before it goes live, and revised over time as facts change.

## Human review and fact-checking

**Liz & Tom Greer**, our Home Solar Writers, oversees editorial quality on Solar Home Planner. Articles are reviewed for:

- **Accuracy** — claims are checked against reputable, current sources.
- **Clarity** — guidance is practical and easy to act on.
- **Usefulness** — each article should genuinely answer the reader's question.

We update articles as facts, prices, products, and best practices change, so the information stays current.

## Accuracy and corrections

We work hard to get things right — but if you spot an error or something out of date, please tell us. Reach us through our [contact page](/contact/) and we'll review and correct it promptly. Reader feedback makes our content better.

## Editorial independence

Our recommendations are our own. Solar Home Planner may earn commissions from affiliate links or display advertising, but that **never** determines which products we recommend or what our articles say. Coverage is based on research and merit, not commercial relationships.

## Not a substitute for professional advice

Our content is for general informational purposes only. For decisions that affect your health, finances, legal situation, or safety, consult a qualified professional who can advise on your specific circumstances.
